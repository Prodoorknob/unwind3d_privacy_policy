Privacy Policy

Unwind 3D: Rope Puzzle  ·  Last updated: March 12, 2026  ·  Effective: March 12, 2026

Short version: Unwind 3D does not collect your name, email, or any personally identifying information. We use third-party SDKs for ads and analytics that collect limited device-level data as described below. You can play entirely offline.

1. Who We Are

Unwind 3D: Rope Puzzle is developed and published by Cobbles & Currents Studios, a sole-developer studio operated by Raj Vedire. For privacy questions, contact us at: privacy@rvedire.com

2. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information. The app stores the following data locally on your device only using Unity PlayerPrefs:

  • Your selected pet and pet name (stored locally)
  • Number of treats remaining (stored locally)
  • Toys collected (stored locally)
  • Current level progress and settings (stored locally)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

3. Third-Party SDKs

The app includes the following third-party SDKs which may collect device-level data for their stated purposes:

AppLovin MAX (Advertising)
Displays advertisements in the app. May collect: device identifiers (Android Advertising ID), IP address, approximate location, device model, OS version, and ad interaction data. This data is used to serve and measure ads.
AppLovin Privacy Policy →

Google Firebase Analytics
Used for anonymous gameplay analytics (e.g. level completion rates, crash reports). May collect: device identifiers, app usage events, and crash logs. Data is aggregated and not linked to your identity.
Firebase Privacy Policy →

Google Firebase Crashlytics
Collects crash reports to help us fix bugs. May collect: device state, stack traces, and device identifiers at the time of a crash.
Firebase Privacy Policy →

Unity In-App Purchasing
Processes in-app purchases (treat packs, no-ads). Payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play — we never see or store your payment information.
Unity Privacy Policy →

4. Advertising & Ad Personalization

Unwind 3D displays ads through AppLovin MAX. These ads may be personalized based on your device's Android Advertising ID (AAID). You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time in your device settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

Ads are never shown during active gameplay. Interstitial ads appear between levels (after every 4 completions). Rewarded ads are optional and player-initiated only (hint system). Banner ads appear on the main menu only.

5. Children's Privacy

Unwind 3D is rated for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not contain social features, user accounts, or any mechanism for children to share personal information. If you believe a child has provided personal data through a third-party SDK, please contact us at privacy@rvedire.com and we will investigate promptly.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Data collected by third-party SDKs is governed by their respective privacy policies (linked above). We do not have access to payment information — all purchases are processed by Google Play.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

Local game data (pet, treats, level progress) is stored on your device and can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app. Analytics data held by Firebase is retained per Google's standard retention policy (up to 14 months for aggregated analytics).

To request deletion of any analytics data associated with your device, contact us at privacy@rvedire.com with your Android Advertising ID and we will submit a deletion request to the relevant SDK providers on your behalf.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • The right to know what data is collected about you
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to opt out of personalized advertising
  • The right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact privacy@rvedire.com.

9. Security

Since we do not collect or store personal data on our own servers, there is no server-side data at risk. Local device data is protected by your device's own security (screen lock, encryption). Third-party SDK data security is governed by the respective providers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. Significant changes will be noted in the app's Play Store update notes.
